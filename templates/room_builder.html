Grid Prototype

Use your mouse to select squares. Clicking a column or row header will select all squares in that row or column.
Hotkeys X, A, and L set attributes to a chair such as "Forbid", "Aisle", and "Left-Handed" respectively.
Holding R while clicking a row or column header will let you rename that row or column.


Project Information

The groupre project is designed to provide a tool for instructional use in large classroom environments where assigning of seats for team-creation or other similar goals is desired.
